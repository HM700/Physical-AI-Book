# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics educational resource! This book is designed for beginners and intermediate learners who want to understand the fundamentals of physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

In this book, you'll explore:

- **Physical AI Concepts**: Understanding how artificial intelligence integrates with physical systems
- **Robotics Fundamentals**: Learning about sensors, actuators, control systems, and perception
- **Humanoid Design**: Exploring the unique challenges and solutions in humanoid robotics
- **Simulation Environments**: Working with ROS 2, Gazebo, Unity, and NVIDIA Isaac
- **Hands-on Exercises**: Practical exercises to reinforce theoretical concepts

## Who This Book Is For

This book is perfect for:

- **Beginners**: Those new to robotics who want a structured introduction
- **Intermediate Learners**: Those with some experience who want to deepen their understanding
- **Students**: Those studying robotics, AI, or related fields
- **Developers**: Those looking to transition into robotics development
- **Researchers**: Those exploring physical AI applications

## How to Use This Book

The book is organized into chapters, each containing multiple lessons. Each lesson includes:

- **Learning Objectives**: What you'll be able to do after completing the lesson
- **Key Concepts**: Theoretical foundations and important principles
- **Hands-on Exercises**: Practical activities to apply what you've learned
- **Summary**: Key takeaways and next steps

We recommend following the lessons in order, as each builds on the previous one. The hands-on exercises are essential for truly understanding the concepts.

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of this book, you should have:

- Basic programming experience (Python recommended)
- Fundamental mathematics knowledge (algebra, basic calculus, linear algebra)
- Access to a computer capable of running simulation environments
- Enthusiasm for robotics and AI!

## Technical Requirements

For the hands-on exercises, you'll need to set up one or more of these simulation environments:

- **ROS 2**: Robot Operating System for robotics development
- **Gazebo**: Physics-based simulation environment
- **Unity**: Game engine for high-quality visual simulation
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: GPU-accelerated simulation and AI tools

Don't worry if you don't have all of these set up initially. We provide setup guides for each platform.

## Getting Started

Ready to begin your journey into physical AI and humanoid robotics? Start with Chapter 1, which introduces the fundamental concepts and gets you set up with your first simulation.

Each lesson is designed to be self-contained while contributing to the broader understanding of physical AI and humanoid robotics. The hands-on exercises will help you apply concepts immediately, reinforcing your learning through practical experience.

Let's begin exploring the exciting world where artificial intelligence meets the physical world!
